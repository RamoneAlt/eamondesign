What Are Game Jams?

game jams are in short, small community held events where people are tasked with making games based around some central theme typically, and then are given a short time period (say... 1 week) to make a game with it. so here are all of my game jam submissions!

Gotta Ketch-Up

This was my first ever game made with unity! it was made in the span of around two weeks during the summer of 2023. i'll be honest, the art is pretty meh, the gameplay is not very well executed, but the gimmick was definitely a fairly unique one. although it's not the best, i was definitely proud of it at the time!

^^^ and this fantastic cover art was made by my sister , an absolute chad who assists me with pretty much all of my game jam games and puts up with my chaos.

Descent To Hell

Descent to Hell is a game I made for the IcoJam 2023. It spanned 3 days, and the theme was "3...2..1." To say i had no idea what to make would be an understatement. but eventually i came up with the idea of a puzzle / platformer type game where the player has 3 total jumps, and built off of that as i went.

i'm not the proudest of this one, the level design is quite MEH, but i did also only have three days

Insync

Insync was made for 2023's 1-Bit Game Jam, so I only was able to use two different colors. in Insync the player takes control of 2 different players, moving them at the same time to go through tricky puzzles and challenges. it has 23 total levels and has to be one of my favorite games i've made

did you know that Diplodocus means "double beam"? the name comes from the strangely shaped chevron bones on the underside of the dino

Out o' Space

This submission to the GBJam 11 was made over the span of 10 days. the theme was space, and i was limited by the gameboy's limitations for this game! Out o' Space is a game where you have to fit oddly shaped aliens into a 12x12 grid, clearing lines and columns as you go!

it didn't perform the best. i recall being very busy with my schoolwork so i didn't have loads of time... but i think the game is pretty fun to play!
