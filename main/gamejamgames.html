What Are Game Jams?

game jams are in short, small community held events where people are tasked with making games based around some central theme typically, and then are given a short time period (say... 1 week) to make a game with it. so here are all of my game jam submissions!

Gotta Ketch-Up

This was my first ever game made with unity! it was made in the span of around two weeks during the summer of 2023. I'll be honest, the art is pretty meh, the gameplay is not very well executed, but the gimmick was definitely a fairly unique one. although it's not the best, i was definitely proud of it at the time!
