how to play!


you start with a word in the center of the screen like "COLD." your goal is to transform this word into the three target words displayed at the top in the least amount of turns possible which you can do in any order you so choose. but the challenge...


on each turn you can do one of the following actions-


  1. change one letter - modify one letter in the current word. for example change "COLD" to "GOLD."


  2. make an anagram - rearrange the letters of the current word to create a new word. for example rearrange the letters from "DUST" to make the word "STUD."

enjoy!
