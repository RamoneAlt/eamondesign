Who are we?

We are a passionate group of 7th graders from Rhode Island dedicated to raising awareness about the water crisis in East Africa. Our mission is to educate people about the challenges millions face in accessing clean water and proper sanitation.

What do we do?

Through our website we inform and empower individuals to make a difference. We provide valuable information and tips to inspire action toward improving water and sanitation conditions. Join us in achieving United Nations Goal #6 for clean water and sanitation for all.
